PAGES
========================
1.Login Page
    -Input field for username.
    -Input field for password.
    -Button for submitting login details.
    -(Optional) Links for "Forgot Password" and "Sign Up".

2.Dashboard
    ->Data Upload Form
        -Fields to input or upload dummy data.
        -Submit button for data upload.
        -Confirmation message after successful upload.

    ->Data Cards
        -Display of uploaded data in card format.
        -Each card showing key details of the data.
        -(Optional) Buttons for editing or deleting data.

    ->Search Bar
        -Input field for searching within the uploaded data.
        -Dynamic display of search results.
        -Message for "No Results Found" if applicable.
